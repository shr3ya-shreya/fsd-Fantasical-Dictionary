Petrichor

📖 Meaning

A pleasant smell that frequently accompanies the first rain after a long period of warm, dry weather.

✨ Example Sentences

🌸 The petrichor filled the air as the summer storm began.

🌸 She stepped outside to breathe in the sweet petrichor.

Synonyms Antonyms
